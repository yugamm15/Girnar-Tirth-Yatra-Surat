# Girnar Tirth Yatra Surat

**Girnar Tirth Yatra Surat** is a dedicated group working for the promotion and facilitation of the Girnar Tirth Yatra, operating under the guidance of **P. Pu. Acharya HemVallabh Suri Ji Maharaj**.

## 🌟 About Us

We are a passionate team committed to serving the Jain community by organizing and supporting the sacred pilgrimage to **Girnar**, one of the most revered holy sites in Jainism. Our mission is to make the journey accessible, comfortable, and spiritually enriching for all devotees.

## 📜 Our Purpose

- **Promote the Yatra**: Encourage and facilitate the pilgrimage to Girnar.
- **Community Service**: Provide support and services to Yatris during their journey.
- **Spiritual Guidance**: Operate under the divine guidance of Acharya HemVallabh Suri Ji Maharaj.

## 🤝 Join Us

We welcome all volunteers, donors, and Yatris who wish to be a part of this noble cause. Your support helps us serve the community better and ensure that the sanctity of the pilgrimage is maintained.
